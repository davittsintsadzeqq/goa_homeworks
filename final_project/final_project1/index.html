Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, click the "Enroll Now" button on the course page and complete the registration form. Our team will contact you with further instructions.

What are the payment options?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and bank transfers. Payment is due monthly, and we offer discounts for full course payments.

Are there any prerequisites for courses?

Most beginner courses have no prerequisites. For advanced courses, basic knowledge of the subject may be required. Each course page includes specific prerequisite information.

How are classes conducted?

Classes are conducted online via Discord. Live sessions include lectures, hands-on exercises, and Q&A. Recorded sessions are available for review, and homework assignments reinforce learning.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, we offer limited scholarships based on merit and need. Contact us for more information about scholarship opportunities and the application process.
